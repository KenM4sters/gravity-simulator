Introduction

In this simulation, two particles of equal mass share the same initial x and z velocities.

Because of this, they're equally attracted to each other and are locked in a symetrical binary orbit, where, at any point in time, they share exactly the same speed.

This simulation assumes zero vertical acceleration, and uses Isaac Newton's law of Universal Gravitation, amongst other formulea to calculate the acceleration vectors of each particle on each frame.

The result is a realistic demonstration of how two particles interact with each other gravitationally.
